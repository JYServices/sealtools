About Length Conversion

This modern and user-friendly length converter allows you to easily convert between various units of length and distance. Simply enter the value, select the original unit, and the desired unit to convert to. The result will be displayed instantly.

Useful for construction, travel planning, scientific calculations, education, or everyday tasks requiring measurement conversions.

Supported Units

  • Meter (m): The base unit of length in the International System of Units (SI).
  • Kilometer (km): Equal to 1,000 meters. Commonly used for geographic distances.
  • Centimeter (cm): Equal to 1/100th of a meter.
  • Millimeter (mm): Equal to 1/1000th of a meter.
  • Mile (mi): Unit of length in the imperial system, equal to 1,609.34 meters.
  • Yard (yd): Imperial unit, equal to 3 feet or 0.9144 meters.
  • Foot (ft): Imperial unit, equal to 12 inches or 0.3048 meters.
  • Inch (in): Imperial unit, equal to 1/12th of a foot or 2.54 centimeters.
  • Nautical Mile (nmi): Used in maritime and air navigation, equal to 1,852 meters.
  • Micrometer (Âµm): Equal to one millionth of a meter.
  • Nanometer (nm): Equal to one billionth of a meter.
