About Temperature Conversion

This modern and user-friendly temperature converter allows you to easily convert between various temperature scales. Simply enter the value, select the original unit, and the desired unit to convert to. The result will be displayed instantly.

Whether you're a scientist, student, traveler, or just curious, this tool provides a quick and accurate way to convert temperatures for various purposes.

Supported Units

  • Celsius (°C): Based on the freezing point of water at 0°C and the boiling point at 100°C.
  • Fahrenheit (°F): Based on the freezing point of water at 32°F and the boiling point at 212°F.
  • Kelvin (K): The absolute thermodynamic temperature scale, with 0 K being absolute zero.
  • Rankine (°R): An absolute thermodynamic temperature scale where zero degrees Rankine is absolute zero. Uses Fahrenheit scale intervals.
  • Delisle (°De): A temperature scale where the boiling point of water is 0 °De and the freezing point is 150 °De.
  • Newton (°N): A temperature scale where the freezing point of water is 0 °N and the boiling point is 33 °N.
  • Réaumur (°Ré): A temperature scale where the freezing point of water is 0 °Ré and the boiling point is 80 °Ré.
  • Rømer (°Rø): A temperature scale where the freezing point of water is 7.5 °Rø and the boiling point is 60 °Rø.
